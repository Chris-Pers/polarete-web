# Welcome to Polarete

This is the first post on our blog. We'll write here about the **philosophy of balance**,
how we build the product, and why a task manager alone is not enough.

## Six areas of life

Polarete assumes you live in six areas at once — health, sports, work, relationships,
play, growth. Each needs attention, but never in equal proportion.
